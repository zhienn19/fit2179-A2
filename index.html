Dengue Cases in Malaysia

Dengue fever remains one of the most persistent and critical public health challenges in Malaysia. As a tropical nation characterized by rapid urbanization, high population density, and a warm humid climate, Malaysia provides an ideal breeding environment for Aedes mosquitoes.
